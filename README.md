## Introduction
The khPOS Corpus (Khmer POS Corpus) is a 12,000 sentences (25,626 words) manually word segmented and POS tagged corpus developed for Khmer language NLP research and developments. We collected Khmer sentences from websites that include various area such as economics, news, politics. Moreover it is also contained some student list and voter list of national election committee of Cambodia. The average number of words per sentence in the whole corpus is 10.75.
